# Data Sources

All datasets used in this analysis are publicly available federal data. No personally identifiable information is included.

## Datasets

| # | Dataset | Agency | Year | Download Link |
|---|---------|--------|------|---------------|
| 1 | National Survey of Children's Health (NSCH) | HRSA / MCHB | 2022 | https://data.census.gov |
| 2 | Youth Risk Behavior Surveillance System (YRBSS) | CDC | 2023 | https://www.cdc.gov/yrbs/ |
| 3 | National Survey on Drug Use and Health (NSDUH) | SAMHSA | 2022-2023 | https://www.samhsa.gov/data/ |
| 4 | Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) | CDC | 2023 | https://www.cdc.gov/brfss/ |
| 5 | American Community Survey (ACS) 5-Year Estimates | U.S. Census Bureau | 2019-2023 | https://data.census.gov |
| 6 | Small Area Health Insurance Estimates (SAHIE) | U.S. Census Bureau | 2022 | https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/sahie.html |
| 7 | Mental Health America (MHA) State Rankings | MHA | 2024 | https://www.mhanational.org/ |
| 8 | Area Health Resources Files (AHRF) | HRSA | 2023 | https://data.hrsa.gov/ |
| 9 | Rural-Urban Continuum Codes (RUCC) | USDA ERS | 2023 | https://www.ers.usda.gov/data-products/rural-urban-continuum-codes/ |
| 10 | SAIPE (Small Area Income and Poverty Estimates) | U.S. Census Bureau | 2022 | https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/saipe.html |
| 11 | National Child Abuse and Neglect Data System (NCANDS) | ACF / HHS | 2022 | https://www.acf.hhs.gov/cb/data-research/child-maltreatment |

## Notes

- ACS data uses 5-year pooled estimates (2019-2023) for county-level reliability
- NSDUH uses 2-year pooled state-level estimates (2022-2023)
- RUCC codes classify each county as metropolitan or non-metropolitan
- All source files are too large for direct inclusion; download from links above
